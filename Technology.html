Traits & Technologies

As farmer needs evolve, so does our research and development pipeline.

Providing farmers the latest traits and technologies to help them be more profitable on every acre is something, as a company, Monsanto prides itself on. It’s more than just helping farmers find the latest advancements in seed – it’s helping farmers find the latest seeds and technologies, while using fewer inputs. Whether it's purchasing seed with the latest traits that help protect that crop's yield, or purchasing seed with a seed treatment to help the seed come out of the ground faster and healthier, Monsanto offers a variety of traits and technologies.
